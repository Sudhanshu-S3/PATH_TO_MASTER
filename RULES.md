## RULES

The rules I hold myself to.

- Have fun in the process.
- Stay clear on the why and the what of whatever I am learning.
- Dig deep instead of rushing through.
- Build it brick by brick.

### Actions

1. Break the work into the smallest tasks I can, and do some every single day.
2. Do not move on until I understand it fully.
3. Keep a 15 day target, split into daily actions. Be realistic about what a day
   actually holds.
4. Start slow. Build momentum first, add more only once it is there.
5. Minimal use of AI.
6. Have fun along the way, not just at the end.
